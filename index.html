Understanding Cancer

Cancer occurs when abnormal cells divide uncontrollably and destroy body tissue. There are over 100 types of cancer, each with unique characteristics and treatment approaches.

Early detection significantly improves outcomes. Knowing the warning signs and risk factors can help with early diagnosis and more effective treatment.

Learn About Treatment Options

Treatment Options

Modern medicine offers multiple approaches to treating cancer. The right option depends on your specific diagnosis.

Standard Cancer Treatments

These are the most common and well-established cancer treatments:

  • Surgery: Physical removal of cancerous tissue
  • Chemotherapy: Powerful drugs that kill cancer cells
  • Radiation Therapy: High-energy rays to destroy cancer cells
  • Hormone Therapy: Blocks hormones that fuel certain cancers

Emerging Cancer Therapies

New treatments showing promise in clinical trials:

  • Immunotherapy: Boosts your immune system to fight cancer
  • Targeted Therapy: Drugs that target specific cancer cell mutations
  • CAR T-Cell Therapy: Genetically modified immune cells
  • Precision Medicine: Treatment based on your genetic profile

Supportive & Holistic Care

Approaches to improve quality of life during treatment:

  • Pain Management: Techniques to control cancer-related pain
  • Nutrition Therapy: Dietary support for strength and recovery
  • Mental Health Support: Counseling and stress management
  • Complementary Therapies: Acupuncture, massage, meditation
